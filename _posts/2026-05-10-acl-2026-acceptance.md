We are glad to announce that our paper  

**“Frame-Semantic Knowledge Injection for Event-Level Inference in LLMs”**  

by **Shahid Iqbal Rai, Danilo Croce, and Roberto Basili**  
has been accepted at **ACL 2026**. 🎉

This work investigates how **frame-semantic knowledge** can be injected into **Large Language Models** to improve their ability to perform **event-level inference**.  
The paper continues our research line on combining **explicit linguistic knowledge**, **semantic roles**, and **neural language models**, with the goal of making LLMs more reliable, controllable, and semantically grounded.

Stay tuned for more details once the official ACL 2026 proceedings are available!
