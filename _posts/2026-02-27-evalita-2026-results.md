We are glad to announce that our **UniTor** systems achieved excellent results at **EVALITA 2026**.

- **Best System** in **Cruciverb-IT**, the task on Italian crossword clue answering.  
- **Best System** in **EVWSD-ITA**, the task on Enhanced Visual Word Sense Disambiguation for Italian.  
- **Honorable Mention** in the “Federico Sangati” Best System Award for **DeSegMa-It**, the task on detection and segmentation of machine-generated texts in Italian.  
- Participation in **PFB**, the task on benchmarking language models in the financial domain.

These results further consolidate our work on retrieval-augmented reasoning, multimodal grounding, machine-generated text detection, and domain-specific question answering for Italian.
